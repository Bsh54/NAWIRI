- **NHIA — National Health Insurance Authority** — overseeing body (premiums GH¢7-42 + GH¢6 card fee, exemptions, ~95% disease coverage, USSD renewal \*929#, MyNHIS App).
- **mogcsp.gov.gh** — Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection (LEAP).
- **leap.mogcsp.gov.gh** — LEAP Management Secretariat (eligibility, bi-monthly amounts GH¢256-424, PMT targeting).

## 5. Key verification notes (corrections applied vs the original brief)

- **Elderly exemption is for 70+** (not just "elderly").
- **Free dialysis** added to the NHIS benefits package since **1 December 2024** — now all NHIS members with kidney disease (originally a 2024 pilot for under-18s/over-60s).
- **NHIS premium corrected:** statutory **GH¢7-42/year + GH¢6 card fee** (not "GHS 30-50"); some district sources quote higher all-in figures.
- **Ghana Card** is the registration prerequisite (NHIS integrated since 2022).
- **One-month waiting period** for new/late-renewing members (temporarily waived Nov → 14 Dec 2025).
- LEAP uses **PMT targeting in selected communities** — beneficiaries are selected, not self-enrolled; LEAP membership grants free NHIS enrolment.
