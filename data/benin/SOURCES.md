## 5. Key verification notes (corrections applied vs the original brief)

- **Entry point is ANPS, not "ANAM / ramubenin.com"** — the latter is outdated. RAMU = legal framework only; ARCH (run by ANPS) is the operational desk.
- **AMO premiums:** children 9,000 / adults 16,000 FCFA/year; employer pays ≥80%; 20% co-payment except the extreme poor.
- ⚠️ **Dialysis and insulin are NOT free in Benin** — only a Sept. 2024 customs/tax exemption on dialysis-kit components. Do not state these are free.
- **ANIP biometric card** is a cross-cutting prerequisite for most programs.
- Many programs use **survey-based targeting (PMT/RSU)** — beneficiaries are selected, not self-enrolled.
