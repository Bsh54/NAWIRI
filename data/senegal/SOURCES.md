## 5. Key verification notes (corrections applied vs the original brief)

- **ANACMU → SEN-CSU** (Decree 2024-832 of 27 March 2024); site **sencsu.sn** (agencecmu.sn still works).
- **PNBSF amount corrected:** 35,000 FCFA/quarter = **140,000 FCFA/year** (since Dec. 2022), not 100,000.
- **CMU rates:** 7,000 FCFA/person/year; 3,500 if 50%-subsidized; free for the indigent identified via the **RNU**.
- **CMU exclusions:** cosmetic surgery, complex dental prosthetics, off-list medicines, medical evacuation abroad.
- **RNU (Registre National Unique)** is the cross-cutting targeting tool — register at the town hall / departmental social services, beneficiaries are selected, not self-enrolled.
- Free-care programs (under-5, Plan Sésame, cesarean, dialysis) apply in **public** facilities.
